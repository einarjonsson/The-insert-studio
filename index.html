Appendix

How to print & assemble

Step one
Choose a tool

Pick from the three tools above. All run entirely in your browser — no account, no install, no data sent anywhere.

Step two
Configure & preview

Set your notebook size (Regular or Passport), adjust difficulty, font, count and personalisation. The live canvas preview updates instantly.

Step three
Print on A4 landscape

Duplex printers produce one PDF. Manual printers get a guided two-step flow — Side 1 first, then flip & print Side 2 with correctly reversed pages.

Step four
Fold, cut & enjoy

Regular: fold at 110mm, cut at 220mm, discard the waste strip. Passport: cut horizontally and vertically at the centre lines. Slip into your TN.
